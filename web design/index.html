    Borobudur Temple is a magnificent ancient structure located in Central Java, Indonesia. Built in the 9th century during the reign of the Sailendra Dynasty, it is the world's largest Buddhist temple and one of the greatest monuments in history.

  • Summer Holiday To The Beautiful Raja Ampat

    Raja Ampat is an archipelago located off the northwest tip of Papua, Indonesia, and is renowned as one of the most breathtaking and biodiverse marine regions in the world. Comprising over 1,500 small islands, cays, and shoals, Raja Ampat is often referred to as a paradise for divers and nature lovers.

  • Kawah Ijen Weekend Vacation

    Kawah Ijen is an extraordinary volcanic crater located in East Java, Indonesia, famous for its stunning blue flames and sulfur mines. The crater is part of the Ijen volcano complex, which features a large caldera with a diameter of about 20 kilometers and several stratovolcanoes. At the heart of Kawah Ijen lies a turquoise-colored acidic crater lake, one of the largest and most acidic in the world.
